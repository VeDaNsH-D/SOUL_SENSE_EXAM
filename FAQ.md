# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

<details>
<summary><strong>Q1. Is this a medical or diagnostic test?</strong></summary>

No. This application is not a medical or psychological diagnostic tool.
It is meant for self-reflection and educational purposes only.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q2. Are my responses stored?</strong></summary>

User responses may be stored securely to improve insights and future features.
Personal data is handled responsibly and with user consent.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q3. Can I retake the exam?</strong></summary>

Yes, users can retake the exam to track changes in their emotional patterns over time.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q4. How are the results calculated?</strong></summary>

Results are generated based on predefined logic and, in future updates, may use data-driven or ML-based analysis.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q5. Can I edit my personal details later?</strong></summary>

Yes, users will be able to update their profile information as new features are added.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q6. Will this app give therapy or treatment advice?</strong></summary>

No. The app may provide general insights or suggestions, but it does not replace professional help.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q7. Is my data shared with others?</strong></summary>

No personal data is shared without consent. Any data used for analysis is anonymized.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Q8. Who should I contact for support or feedback?</strong></summary>

You can raise an issue on the GitHub repository or contact the project maintainers through the community channels.
</details>

<br>

---
*If you have any other questions, please reach out to the development team.*
